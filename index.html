    Our Story & Vision

    Established in 2002, FTX Global Investment LLC has evolved from a specialized freight forwarding company into a diversified global powerhouse. Based in the strategic hub of Dubai, we bridge the gap between logistics expertise and international investment. Our journey is defined by a passion for efficiency, safety, and a commitment to fueling growth across borders.

    • Global Logistics & Supply Chain Management
    • Strategic Business Setup & Investment in Dubai
    • Official Sponsors of Formula 4 Racing
    • Empowering Youth through F4 Motorsport Sponsorship
    • International Standards (ISO 9001, 14001 & OHSAS 18001)

    With over two decades of operational experience, we provide our clients with a reliable infrastructure and a guarantee of uncompromising quality. At FTX, we don't just move goods or manage investments; we drive the future.

    Global Logistics & Supply Chain

    Expertise in Long-Haul & Distribution Leveraging over two decades of experience since our founding in 2002, we specialize in comprehensive supply chain management. From countrywide distribution of spares to large-scale freight forwarding, our infrastructure ensures that every logistical need is met with precision and Resourceful facilities. We bridge the gap between complex logistics and efficient delivery across all borders.

    Specialized Fuel Transportation

    Safe Movement of Petroleum Products Through our subsidiary, Fast Energy, we provide specialized tanker services for the movement of various petroleum products. As an SQAS-assessed carrier, we meet all stringent requirements for handling flammable and dangerous goods as mandated by the Department of Energy. We prioritize high-level safety protocols to ensure the integrity of essential energy resources.

    Precision Mining Operations

    Advanced Underground Resource Logistics FTX is a leader in the mining sector, specializing in the complex logistics required for deep-level underground environments. We utilize our specialized plant equipment and 32-ton horse and trailer fleet to manage the seamless extraction and transport of ore. Our operations are grounded in international ISO standards for quality and safety to support the rigorous demands of global mining operations.

    Heavy Haulage & Abnormal Loads

    Moving the Industry’s Largest Assets We provide expert low-bed and abnormal transportation services designed specifically for heavy machinery and industrial plant equipment. Our fleet meets all road transport legal requirements and is meticulously maintained by our technical teams to avoid breakdowns and delivery delays. We have the financial capability to acquire specialized trailers tailored to your project's specific heavy-haulage needs.

    Coal & Energy Supply Chain

    Reliable Partnerships with Blue-Chip Entities Since acquiring a major contract with ESKOM in 2007, FTX has been a leading supplier in coal transportation. We manage large-scale energy supply chains for partners such as ROTRAN and AFGRI, focusing on the safe and efficient delivery of bulk resources. Our goal is to maintain these blue-chip contracts through a passion for excellence and uncompromising quality.

    Formula 4 & Motorsport Excellence

    Driving Innovation and Youth Development Our commitment to precision extends to the racetrack as the Official Sponsor of Formula 4 Racing. This partnership reflects the core values of FTX: speed, engineering excellence, and data-driven performance. By backing F4, we are dedicated to empowering the next generation of athletic talent, applying the same high-performance standards to motorsport that we use in our global logistics operations.
